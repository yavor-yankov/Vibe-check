## Key Differentiators

1. **Socratic interview, not a form** — the AI asks 5–7 questions based on your specific idea, not a generic template. Competitors (ValidatorAI, IdeaProof, IdeaScan) use static forms.

2. **Live competitor scan** — uses Tavily to search the web in real time. Other tools either skip this or generate hallucinated competitors from training data.

3. **Devil's Advocate mode** — the report explicitly argues against your strongest points. No other major validator has this feature.

4. **Refine & Re-score** — founders can iterate on the same idea and see scores change. The only tool with in-session iteration.

5. **Price** — unlimited at $9/month vs. ValidatorAI ($29/mo), IdeaProof ($19/mo), IdeaScan ($15/check).

## Feature Overview

### The 4-Stage Wizard Flow

**Stage 1 — Intro**
Founder describes their idea in a text field. No forms, no dropdowns.

**Stage 2 — Interview**
AI runs 5–7 turns of Socratic back-and-forth. It pushes on specifics: who the customer is, why existing solutions don't work, what would make the idea fail.

**Stage 3 — Scanning**
Tavily searches the web for real competitors and similar products. Results cached for 7 days to reduce cost and latency.

**Stage 4 — Report**
Scored across 6 dimensions with full reasoning. A one-word verdict (Strong / Promising / Iterate / Rethink / Avoid). Devil's Advocate toggle shows the counterargument. "Refine & Re-score" button lets founders update their answers and see how the score changes.
